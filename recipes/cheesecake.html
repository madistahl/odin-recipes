Cheesecake

Description

The best cheesecake recipe.

Ingredients

Crust

  • 1 1/4 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup sugar

Filling

  • 2 pkg cream cheese, softened
  • 2 large eggs, room temperature
  • 2/3 cup sugar, divided
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract, divided
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs, butter, and sugar. Pat onto the bottom and up the sides of a 9-in pie plate. Chill.
  2. For filling, beat cream cheese and eggs in a bowl on medium speed for 1 minute. Add 1/3 cup sugar, 1 tsp vanilla, and salt. Continue beating until well blended, about 1 minute. Pour into crust.
  3. Bake at 350 for 35 minutes. Cool for 10 minutes.
  4. For topping, combine the sour cream, 1/3 cup sugar, and 1 tsp vanilla; spread over cheesecake.
  5. Bake for 10 minutes, cool completely on a wire rack. Refrigerate at least 3 hours.
