Cutout cookies

Description

My grandma's recipe for Christmas cookies.

Ingredients

Cookies

  • 3 cups sifted all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3 tablespoons sour cream

Frosting

  • 6 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 3 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon almond extract
  • 2-4 tablespoons milk

Steps

  1. Sift flour and salt together, set aside.
  2. Cream butter well, add sugar gradually and continue creaming until light and fluffy.
  3. Add and mix beaten eggs and extracts to creamed mixture.
  4. Dissolve baking soda in sour cream.
  5. Add sifted dry ingredients alternately with sour cream mixture to the butter mixture.
  6. Chill dough about 2 hours.
  7. Roll to 1/8 inch thickness on floured board or pastry cloth and cut with floured cookie cutters.
  8. Bake on un-greased cookie sheet at 350 for 10-12 minutes. Cool.
  9. Make frosting & decorate!
