Pumpkin Cheesecake

Description

This recipe combines my normal recipe with pumpkin.

Ingredients

Crust

  • 1 1/4 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup sugar

Filling

  • 2 pkg cream cheese, softened
  • 2 large eggs, room temperature
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Pumpkin topping

  • 1/2 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1 tsp pumpkin pie spice
  • Cinnamon to taste

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs, butter, and sugar. Pat onto the bottom and up the sides of a 9-in pie plate. Chill.
  2. For filling, beat cream cheese and eggs in a bowl on medium speed for 1 minute. Add sugar, vanilla, and salt. Continue beating until well blended, about 1 minute. Add 1 cup filling to crust.
  3. Add pumpkin and spices, stir gently, layer onto cheesecake.
  4. Bake at 350 for 35 minutes.
  5. Cool completely on a wire rack. Refrigerate at least 3 hours.
